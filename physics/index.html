About

DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICAL SCIENCES - A DECADE OF EXCELLENCE (2007-2020)

Since its inception, the department has offered students a stimulating and creative atmosphere. With a variety of elective options, the department offers a Master of Science (MSc) in Physics. The department also offers a PhD programme in the cutting-edge fields of physics and material sciences. The final semester of MSc includes a full semester of research projects. The course content is created in accordance with UGC requirements.


Thrust Areas @ Department
  • Condensed Matter Theory and Experiment
  • Functional Nanostructured Materials, Thin Films, and Devices
  • Magnetic Nanoparticles and MR Fluid
  • Theoretical High Energy Physics and Astrophysics
